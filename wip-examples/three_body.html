# The Three-Body Problem

The Three-Body Problem is a science fiction novel written by Chinese author Liu Cixin, the first book in the "Remembrance of Earth's Past" trilogy.

## Synopsis

During the Cultural Revolution, astrophysicist Ye Wenjie is brought to a secret military project called "Red Coast" after surviving numerous hardships. Using the sun as an antenna, Ye Wenjie sends Earth's first signal into the universe, making a breakthrough in the search for extraterrestrial civilization.
